The Transformation

More than implementation.
A new operating model.

Most AI vendors build something, hand it over, and leave. Six months later the system sits unused because no one inside the organization knows how to run it, adapt it, or trust it.

ST:AI works differently. Every engagement includes developing an internal AI Ops Manager — someone already on your team who becomes the ongoing steward of your new systems. They learn alongside the build. They own it when we step back.

The result is an organization with a new operational layer and the internal capability to evolve it. That's what transformation actually looks like.

How We Work

A structured path from current state
to operational future.

01

Baseline Sprint

We map how your organization actually works — the tools, the workflows, the handoffs, the gaps. Thirty days of structured discovery produces a clear picture of where agentic systems will deliver the most value, fast.

Duration: 30 days

02

Build and Activate

We design and implement the agent infrastructure specific to your operation. Coordination, communication, contract management, reporting, follow-up — the functions consuming your team's time become automated, auditable, and scalable.

Duration: 60–90 days

03

Capability Transfer

Your designated AI Ops Manager trains alongside the build. By the time we step back, they have the knowledge, the documentation, and the confidence to run, adapt, and extend the system independently.

Outcome: ongoing internal ownership
