How We're Built

SmokingTigers.Enterprises runs as a cooperative. Contributors are partners with a stake in outcomes, sharing directly in the value they create. Revenue flows to the people doing the work. Every engagement is governed by the same principles we help our clients build into their own organizations.

This model attracts a specific kind of professional and a specific kind of client. The ones who know that how the work gets done matters as much as whether it gets done.
