SmokingTigers.Enterprises runs as a cooperative. Contributors are partners with a stake in outcomes, not vendors on a roster. Revenue flows to the people doing the work. Every engagement is governed by the same principles we help our clients build into their own organizations.

This model attracts a specific kind of professional and a specific kind of client. The ones who know that how the work gets done matters as much as whether it gets done.
