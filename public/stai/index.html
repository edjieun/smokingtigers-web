What we do

A thinking partnership.
Your operation,
our architecture.

ST:AI Advisory begins with deep discovery. We understand how decisions are made in your practice, where time disappears, what the regulatory and compliance constraints are, and where AI integration generates genuine value — and we do this before we recommend anything.

From there, we construct the engagement together. The scope reflects your actual situation, built from the Discovery Brief. Delivery is led by a named ST:AI consultant, accountable to you from first conversation to handoff.

Specialist capacity is drawn from the Q1 network per engagement: AI engineers, workflow designers, governance architects, operations strategists — the right people for your specific problem, assembled for your engagement.

At close, you own everything. The systems, the documentation, the governance records, the infrastructure. ST:AI Advisory is designed toward your independence — toward a practice that runs what was built, without ongoing reliance on us to run it.

How we work

Everything is documented and auditable

Every ST:AI engagement runs on GovRepo governance infrastructure. Decisions are logged. Contributors are attributed. IP created during the engagement is documented. The practice receives a complete record of what was built and how — transparent, auditable, and fully yours.

Specialist capacity assembled per engagement

We draw from the Q1 professional services network: AI engineers, workflow designers, governance architects, and operations strategists. You get the right people for your specific problem, not generalists from a bench. Contributor agreements are in place before work begins.

Designed toward your independence

Every engagement includes a transition plan agreed at scoping. At close, the practice self-manages what was built, with full documentation and operational guides. The outcome belongs to you permanently — a fixed scope, fully transferred, with no ongoing dependency.
